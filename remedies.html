Osteoporosis Remedies

Here are some general remedies and preventive measures for osteoporosis:

  • Increase calcium intake through diet or supplements
  • Ensure adequate vitamin D intake
  • Engage in weight-bearing exercises regularly
  • Quit smoking and limit alcohol consumption
  • Maintain a healthy body weight
  • Consider hormone therapy (consult with your doctor)
  • Take medications as prescribed by your healthcare provider
